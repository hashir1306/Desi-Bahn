How we work

Project Brief & Inquiry

We begin by understanding your goals, vision, timeline, and budget. Once the project scope, date, and location are confirmed, we move forward with planning and scheduling.

1
2

Consultation & Strategy

An in-depth discussion to define creative direction, brand positioning, and content requirements. This includes technical guidance and a customized production plan.

Creative Alignment

We collaborate with you to finalize the concept, visual style, deliverables, and key production details to ensure everything aligns with your brand vision.

3
4

Agreement & Initial Payment

The project is formalized through an agreement or invoice. An advance payment (typically 30–50%) is required to reserve production resources and begin development.

Creative Development

Concept creation, scripting, storyboarding, and mood boards are prepared (if required). Final timeline and production schedule are approved by the client.

5
6

Pre-Production & Filming

Our team handles full production setup including crew, equipment, locations, models/talent, styling, and logistics. Filming is executed professionally and efficiently.

Post-Production & Review

Editing starts immediately after filming. A first draft is shared for your feedback, and revisions are made until the final version is approved.

7
8

Final Delivery & Payment

Upon final approval, the remaining payment is completed and all deliverables are shared in high resolution through your preferred delivery method.
